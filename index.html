A little bit of me

I’m Maria Agustina Roig, I am a Graphic Designer since 2004, Once I graduated from the University of Buenos Aires, I began my path in graphic and digital design, working mainly on web pages, designing but also coding, especially coding lately.

I have worked for design studios in Buenos Aires, getting to work for famous brands such as Disney or McDonald’s among others.

Then I made my own way working as a freelance for a while and after a few years I started working remotely for a language learning company that has its main building in the US, for whom I still work.

I lived all my life in Buenos Aires, Argentina, a city I love, but a year ago I moved to the United States, making Tennessee my new home now.

After many years of experience, working with a lot of HTML and CSS and a bit of Javascript, I decided to give my career a new focus, and started a certification in the MITxPro program “Professional full stack developer with MERN”, certification that I am studying at the moment, here seriously begins my path towards programming.
